History of Says International School

Says International School is located in Accra, Ghana. The school was established in September 2019 to provide daily education to children living in Ghana and children of foreign based families.
The school begun with a population of 105 students and currently has students of 400 in attendance.
The school is basically science-oriented with state-of-the-art teaching and learning facilities. The school has two blocks within the same area of school location; Pre-school and Primary section located at the main campus whiles the other block that contains upper primary and JHS all located at west hills.
We have facilities such as a three-storey classroom block, with a canteen, sick bay, library, Montessori center, computer laboratory and many more.
