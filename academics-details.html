Academic Details

Infant School
Creche (3 months - 2 years)

• Infant school - Akwaaba!
We wish to welcome every Parents/Guardain to Says international Infant school homePage. At SIS, Our infant School provides education to children aged between 3 months - 2 years respecfully. the following outlines the infant school class categorization.

• Babies: Well trained teachers are engaged to handle children at the age of 6 months old, Most babies at this stage are able to sit up without the support of anyone and are able to sit well on table and chairs. With this, Says international school has the facility for every baby to play more physical games such as Peekaboo, Tummy-time, singing, kicking, clapping, and many others.

• Toddlers/Infant: Our well trained teachers are able to facilitate early child education for toddlers in the most effective way. Our curriculum ensures Toddlers are always learning by doing, touching, moving, pushing and listening to develop understanding in every academic work they come across with. this is off much interest to us as a school since it will foster their development in studying.

Pre-School
Nursery 1 - Kindergarten 2 (3 - 5 years)

• Our Pre-school, centers on children from (3) three to (5) five years of age. At Say International School, our kindergarten school is most preferred to be the first year of schooling of a child either Boy/Girl who is five years old.

• Our Nursery School is as well considered as a crucial stage in every child's life at Says international school. Reason being that at this stage; a child is ready to be absorbed with lots of activities and studying to improve his/her knowledge in reading and writing skills.

• Activities such as learning by play and in-built classroom learning facilities are emphasized much by the school and our well-trained teachers in every nursery class has a unique learning style. Our Nursery class curriculum is design to let children understand the concepts of studies and school. Tailor-made facilities for children in this category.

Lower Primary
Class 1 - 3 (6 - 8years)

Our lower primary school is between the ages of 6 – 8 years. At Says, Classroom lessons are highly placed and preparations towards each class lessons are carefully prepared by our well-trained teachers to achieve maximum result.

Most of our class room activities stresses on the vital concepts in the day-to-day learning episodes in every class room. In this regard, promoting an order of thinking among themselves (Students) are the way we go to foster brighter future in academic excellence.

One of our class room objectives is teaching Language and Literacy that reinforce knowledge, skills and understanding required to connect students effectively. At the Lower Primary, studying languages like French, English and, Ashanti Twi as subjects presents chances for pupils to become increasingly familiar with the sounds and written form of it.

Upper Primary
Class 4 - 6 (9 - 12years)

Setting a right foundation at the upper primary school level is very paramount towards the growth of our school. We emphasis on the knowledge that was acquired at the lower primary and applied the new learning skills at the upper level.

In this regard, having knowledge on answering techniques and learning examination skills is the focus of every upper primary student of our school. This has enabled most students or group of students get involved to resolved compound questions with the learning skill they have acquired at the upper primary stage of their academic life.

The content of our school curriculum has what it takes to let student engage and master every subject that is taught in the classroom.

Junior High School
JHS 1 - JHS3 (13 - 15 years)

Our Junior high school is between the ages of 11 – 14 years. At SIS, classroom facilities available are well structed and located at a serene and spacious complex to enable students engage in good academic discourse towards their BECE examinations.

At this stage of their academic journey, Junior high school students receive much support from their teachers on how to achieve academic excellence through guidance and critical learning skills towards every level of their class and external examinations.
