Admission

Enrollment Requirement

1. You will be examined after a successful interview.
2. The Examination will be in four (4) parts:
• English
• Mathematics
• General Science
• Social Studies

3. Purchase the application form, complete and submit it to the admission office.
4. Please attach to the completed forms your child

• Last two Terminal Reports / Transcript or Testimonial from the last school.
• Known examination results
• Two (2) passport size photographs • General Science.
• Photocopy of birth certificate or passport.
• Medical Report in the school's format.
