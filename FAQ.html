Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission procedure at SIS?

For Parents/Guidance seeking admissions, an informal interaction will be conducted in which pupil and both the parents/Guidance have to be present. The final decision of the admission committee will be binding.

What is the fee policy/structure?

Please call the school for fee details or refer to the school fee policy contained in the admission page.

What curriculum will SIS follow?

SIS follows GES curriculum.

What will be the school timings?

School timings are: 7:30 am to 3:00 pm.

Will the school authorities be taking adequate measures to ensure hygiene in the school?

SIS maintains a high standard of cleanliness and hygiene. There are regular checks and monitoring by the school administration as well as the Head Teacher of the school.

What is the kind of security offered to the students?

The school has installed CCTV all over the campus. Students are always accompanied by teachers when they are moving from one block to the other.

How many terms will the school have?

We will be having THREE TERMS as per GES school proceedings.

Other than a PTA, when can a parent/guidance interact with the teacher?

SIS considers parents as partners in the education process and parents are free to meet teachers, with prior appointment, as and when necessary.

How many students will be there per division?

We strive to maintain a ratio of 30 Students in each class.
